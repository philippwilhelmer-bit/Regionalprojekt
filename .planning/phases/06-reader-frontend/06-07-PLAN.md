<task type="checkpoint:human-verify" gate="blocking">
  <what-built>
    Complete reader frontend — Bezirk selector modal, personalized feed, article detail pages, RSS feeds, sitemap, Impressum, AdSense integration, SEO metadata, JSON-LD schema.
  </what-built>
  <how-to-verify>
    Start the dev server: `npx next dev`
    Open http://localhost:3000 in a browser (use DevTools responsive mode for mobile).

    **Success Criterion 1 — Bezirk selection persists (READ-01):**
    - Open http://localhost:3000 in a private/incognito window
    - Confirm the Bezirk selection modal appears automatically
    - Select "Liezen" and click "Übernehmen"
    - Refresh the page — confirm Liezen is still shown as selected in the header
    - Clear localStorage and reload — confirm modal appears again

    **Success Criterion 2 — Filtered feed (READ-02):**
    - With Liezen selected, confirm feed shows "Mein Bezirk" heading
    - With no Bezirk selected, confirm feed shows "Alle Nachrichten"
    - Tap a Bezirk badge on an article card → confirm navigates to /bezirk/[slug]

    **Success Criterion 3 — Article detail page (READ-03):**
    - Click any article → confirm /artikel/[publicId]/[slug] URL
    - Confirm: title, full body, source attribution ("Quelle: OTS.at" or RSS), publication timestamp, AI disclosure label (if applicable)
    - Visit the URL with a wrong slug (change last segment) → confirm 301 redirect to correct URL

    **Success Criterion 4 — Mobile optimized (READ-04):**
    - Set DevTools viewport to 375×812 (iPhone SE)
    - Scroll all pages (homepage, article detail, /bezirk/[slug], /impressum)
    - Confirm NO horizontal scroll bar appears on any page
    - Confirm bottom nav is visible on all pages
    - Confirm article cards are readable without zooming

    **Success Criterion 5 — RSS + Impressum (READ-05, READ-06):**
    - Visit http://localhost:3000/rss/liezen.xml — confirm XML response with &lt;rss> root element and &lt;item> entries containing canonical /artikel/ URLs
    - Visit http://localhost:3000/rss/steiermark.xml — confirm valid RSS feed
    - Visit http://localhost:3000/impressum — confirm page has Impressum heading, KI-disclosure section, and Datenschutzerklärung section

    **Success Criterion 6 — SEO meta tags (SEO-01, SEO-03, SEO-04):**
    - On any article page, right-click → View Page Source → confirm:
      - &lt;meta property="og:title"> present
      - &lt;meta property="og:description"> present
      - &lt;link rel="canonical"> with /artikel/[publicId]/[slug] URL
      - &lt;script type="application/ld+json"> with NewsArticle @type present
    - Visit http://localhost:3000/sitemap.xml — confirm XML with homepage, /impressum, /bezirk/[slug], and /artikel/ URLs

    **Success Criterion 7 — AdSense zones (AD-01):**
    - On homepage, confirm a placeholder ad div appears above the feed (hero zone)
    - After every 5 article cards, confirm another placeholder ad appears
    - On article detail page, confirm a placeholder ad appears after the article body
    - Confirm cookie consent banner appears on first visit (after clearing localStorage)
    - Click "Nur notwendige Cookies" → confirm banner dismisses and does not reappear on reload
  </how-to-verify>
  <resume-signal>Type "approved" if all 7 criteria pass, or describe specific failures so they can be fixed.</resume-signal>
