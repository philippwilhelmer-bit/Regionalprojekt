   e. `groupArticlesByBezirk(articles: ArticleWithBezirke[])` — pure function (no DB), returns `Map<string, { name: string, articles: ArticleWithBezirke[] }>`. Group each article by its first bezirk slug. Articles with `isStateWide=true` appear in every group. Articles with no bezirke are excluded. Export this function.
